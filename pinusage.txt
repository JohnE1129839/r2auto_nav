For Servo:
 - 3.3 V pin for Vcc
 - Ground Pin
 - GPIO12 for PWM

For motor & driver:
 - 12V vcc from OpenCR
 - Ground pin
 - GPIO 18 for PWM (Orange)
 - GPIO 23 for normal (Yellow)
 - 2 pins for switch (Pending GPIOs)

For Line Follower:
 - 5V vcc pin
 - Ground pin
 - s1 = 13;
 - s2 = 17;
 - s3 = 19;
 - s4 = 20;
 - s5 = 21;
 - s6 = 22;
 - s7 = 26;
 - s8 = 27;
